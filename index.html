About Me

I’m Edwin Trejo, a Graduate Machine Learning Researcher and M.S. Computer Science candidate at The University of Texas at El Paso, specializing in Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence.

During my cybersecurity internship at Sandia National Laboratories, I contributed to scalable NLP pipelines and real-time detection systems for industrial networks.

I enjoy designing solutions that bridge research and real-world impact — combining data-driven methods, machine learning, and security engineering to make systems safer and smarter. Beyond research, I mentor computer science students, contribute to campus tech initiatives, and explore new ways to apply AI responsibly in critical domains.
